What is Rock Music?

Rock music is a genre that focuses mostly on intrumentals. Guitars, drums, and voals make for a great mixture of sounds. Rock music is very energtic and in known for having guitar solos.

Top Five Rock Artist

Red Hot Chili Peppers

The Red Hot Chilli Peppers are a very popular band in California. They started in Los Angeles, CA. They have six Grammy Awards. Their regarded as the most successful alternative rock band. They rose to fame in the early 9os with their "Blood Sugar Sex Magik" album. Then they released "Californication" which is their most popular album and really put them on the map. Their made up of Anthony Kiedis the lead singer, Flea the bass guitarist, John Frusciante the lead guitarist, and Chad Sith the drummer. Their music combines funk, punk, rock, and rap. Their music varies quite a bit. They are very high energy.

Mötley Crüe

Mötley Crüe is your typical 80s rock band. High energy, electric, and giant hair. They follow the format of electric guitars, drums, and vocals. Their eccentric lifestyles gained them quite a bit of popularity. They were formed in Los Angeles, California in 1981. They shocked the world by showing how they don't care what others thought. They would do what they want. Some people liked this and others hated it. Their members include Tommy Lee, Mick Mars, Nikki Sixx, Vince Neil, and John Corabi.

AC/DC

AC/DC is one of the most popular bands of all time. Their name, AC/DC which stands for "alternating current/direct current" highlights their electric style. They are a hard rock band that uses electric guitars, drums, and more. They dress up in nice clothes on stage and play rock music which contrasts making them unique. They are from Sydney, Australia in 1973. Their album "Highway to Hell" in 1979 rose them to fame. People began to see them as legends of rock. Their members include ANgus Young the lead guitarist, Brian Johnson the lead singer, Bon Scott, Malcolm Yound the rhythm guitar, and Phil Rudd the drummer.

Queen

Queen is probaly the most well known band of all time. They are a classic rock band that is great at live performances, has great vocals, and of course, big hair. Their emotion and energy gives their band a personality and made them popular. Their band consists of Brian May the guitarist, John Deacon the bass guitarist, Roger Taylor the drummer, Mike Gross, and the legendary vocalist Freddy Mercury. Freddy is possibly the most musically talented person to ever exist. His vocal fit their bands style so well. When he came out as gay it shocked the world. He eventually got aids and died in 1991 which was a tragic event.

Nirvana

Nirvana was created in 1987. They are a lithium and grunge band and known for their violent style. Some would say they scream into their microphones to produce music. This style however sounds great. When listening to their music you begin to feel a rush through your body. Although their lyrics are hard to understand, they are very well thought out. They sing about social issues and have strong feelings about these subjects. The name "Nirvana" means to make suffering go away. Their music however, does not give this feel. Their music feels very rough and does not make one feel as if they are reaching Nirvana. They rose to fame with their 1991 album "Nevermind." Their band members include Dave Grohl, Krist Novoselic, and Kurt Cobain who tragically passed away due to suicide. This allowed him to join the 27 club of celebrities who died at 27.
