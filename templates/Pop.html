Eminem, a very successful rapper, has won 44 grammies. There is a movie about him called "8 Mile". His music is about his life, coming from a poor family and a violent neighborhood.

Drake

Drake is one of the biggest rappers in the world. His has many different types of songs and lots of albums. Drake's music is mostly about his past love life.

Lil Uzi Vert, a triple grammy award winner, is a great artist and creates great songs. Influenced by the rapper Mike Jones, he has created many different great albums and singles. His songs are usually about his life through his youger years.
