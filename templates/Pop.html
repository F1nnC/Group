What is Pop Music?

Pop music originates in the United States and the United Kingdom around the 1950s. Pop music has good rhythm and a catchy melody.

Bruno Mars is one of the most popular pop artist and is compared to Michael Jackson, his music is very expansive. His early music focused on heartbreak whilst his music now focuses on happy club-ish music.

Katy Perry is very unique pop artist and has won many awards for her songs. She mostly sings about past relationships and is compared to Lady Gaga.

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift is one of if not the best selling artist of all time.

Rihanna

Rihanna is a very popular pop artist. She was inspired by Janet Jackson, Madonna, and Bob Marley. She is compared to Beyonce.
